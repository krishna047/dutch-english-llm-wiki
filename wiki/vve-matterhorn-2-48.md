# VvE Matterhorn 2–48

**English name:** Homeowners’ association (VvE — *Vereniging van Eigenaren*) **Matterhorn 2–48**, located in **Amstelveen**, Netherlands.

## Role in this wiki

The `raw/` folder holds Dutch association documents (minutes, budgets, annual accounts, deeds, MJOP extracts, etc.). This page is the **hub** for English summaries about the building and its governance.

## Cost model (high level)

Owners pay a **periodic contribution** (*periodieke bijdrage*), often collected as **monthly prepayments** (*voorschotten*). Total building expenses for a year are set out in the **budget** (*begroting*) and later reported in the **annual accounts** (*jaarrekening*). Many lines are split using a **share fraction** (*breukdeel*) per apartment right (*appartementsrecht*).

See [[periodic-contribution-and-breukdeel]] and [[utilities-nutsvoorzieningen-water-electricity]].

# Periodic contributions and *breukdeel*

## Terms

| Dutch (as in sources) | English |
|------------------------|---------|
| *Periodieke bijdrage* | Periodic owner contribution (service charge) |
| *Voorschot* / *Bedragen te voldoen 1 maal per maand* | Monthly prepayment amount |
| *Breukdeel* | Share fraction used to allocate common costs |
| *Appartementsrecht* | Apartment right (unit + schedule reference) |
| *Verdeelsleutel* | Allocation key |

## 2026 budget snapshot (from `raw/783-begroting-2026.md`)

- **Budget period:** 1 January 2026 – 31 December 2026.
- **Total budgeted expenses** (*Totaal* expenditure block): **€109,015.00** for the line items shown on page 1 of that extract.
- **Typical full unit (10 *breukdelen*):** **€4,360.60 per year** for the listed bundle of general costs (insurance, maintenance lines, utilities share, admin, bank, board, major maintenance reserve top-up — **combined**, not water alone).
- **Typical monthly prepayment** for a standard apartment row in the owner list: **€363.38 / month** (matches €4,360.60 ÷ 12).
- **Garage spaces** (*Garage*): often **1** *breukdeel* → **€436.06 / year** and **€36.34 / month** in the same document’s examples.

Owners with **multiple** rights (e.g. apartment + garage) show **summed** monthly totals in the source.

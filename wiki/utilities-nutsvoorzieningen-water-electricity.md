# Shared utilities (nutsvoorzieningen)

In these documents, **“water” is not a personal drinking-water supplier invoice.** It is a **line item in the association’s accounts**: common-building supply or allocation of **utilities** (*nutsvoorzieningen*), split across owners via the same **share-fraction** (*breukdeel*) rules as other general costs.

> **Inference (not a verbatim definition from one clause):** the exact physical scope (e.g. common taps vs allocation method) should be confirmed from deeds or supplier contracts in `raw/` if needed.

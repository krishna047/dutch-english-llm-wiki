# Akte van Splitsing

> **English Summary:** Notarial deed of division (Akte van Splitsing) for the apartment building at Matterhorn 2-48, Amstelveen, executed on January 22, 1973 by notary Willem Jan Mekking in Amsterdam. The deed divides the property (cadastral section L, number 1614) into 34 apartment rights: 24 residential apartments (numbers 1-24, Matterhorn 2-48 even numbers) across four floors, and 10 garages (numbers 25-34) on the ground floor. The building was constructed by Bouwbedrijf Netjes B.V. The deed references the Model Regulations of 1972 with specific amendments regarding radiators, usage restrictions, voting rights (250 total votes, 10 per apartment, 1 per garage), and ownership fractions (10/250 per apartment, 1/250 per garage).
